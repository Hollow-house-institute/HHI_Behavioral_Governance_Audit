# Governance Drift Report

## Finding
The simulation shows Governance Drift between documented human oversight and execution-time behavior.

## Evidence
The pharmacy platform generated an escalation trigger, but the signal did not persist across the scheduling workflow. The human clinician retained formal Stop Authority but did not receive full Interaction Trace context.

## HHI Control
- Governance Telemetry
- Interaction Trace continuity
- Decision Boundary enforcement
- Stop Authority preservation
- Continuous Assurance

## Audit Conclusion
Post-Hoc Governance cannot contain Longitudinal Risk when escalation evidence does not persist during execution.
